<section class="section section--olive-light">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-header">
      <span class="eyebrow">How It Works</span>
      <h2>Operations & model</h2>
      <p>Small cohorts. Deep focus. One competency at a time. The work is not about exposure — it's about embedding.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="two-col" style="margin-bottom:3rem;">
      <div>
        <h3 style="margin-bottom:1rem;">Core structure</h3>
        <p style="color:var(--mid); margin-bottom:0.75rem;">We create cohorts of 4–5 CBOs and deep-dive into a single organizational backbone competency. Total cohorts in 2026–27: <strong>4</strong>.</p>
        <p style="color:var(--mid);">Each organisation commits <strong>3–5 hours per week</strong> and a minimal participation fee (₹500–2,000) to ensure ownership and accountability.</p>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h3 style="margin-bottom:1rem;">CBO selection</h3>
        <p style="color:var(--mid); margin-bottom:0.75rem;">CBOs are selected through referrals from trusted ally organisations connected with community-led founders.</p>
        <p style="color:var(--mid);">Each cohort assigns one youth intern (GenZ team member) to provide implementation support to 2 organisations, expanding their capacity bandwidth.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="phases">
      <div class="phase">
        <span class="phase__badge">Phase 1 · 6–8 weeks</span>
        <h4>Co-Develop</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Group sessions with peer CBOs</li>
          <li>One-on-one implementation support</li>
          <li>Practical assignments for actual tool adoption</li>
          <li>Build the system together inside the org</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="phase">
        <span class="phase__badge" style="background:var(--olive);">Phase 2 · 4–6 weeks</span>
        <h4>Embed</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Structured accountability check-ins</li>
          <li>Practice, troubleshooting, reinforcement</li>
          <li>Solve real friction points as they arise</li>
          <li>Infrastructure stays in the org after we leave</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
