<section class="section bg-olive-pale">
  <div class="container">
    <p class="label">How the model works</p>
    <h2 style="margin:0.8rem 0 1rem;">Operations</h2>
    <p style="color:var(--text-light); max-width:600px; margin-bottom:2.5rem;">
      By creating small cohorts of 4–5 CBOs, we deep-dive into a core organisational backbone competency — one at a time — with a focus not on exposure to tools, but on embedding infrastructure inside the organisation.
    </p>

    <div class="phases">
      <div class="phase">
        <span class="phase-badge">Phase 1 · Co-Develop · 6–8 weeks</span>
        <h4>Building together</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Group sessions with peer CBOs</li>
          <li>One-on-one implementation support</li>
          <li>Practical assignments for tool adoption</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="phase">
        <span class="phase-badge">Phase 2 · Embed · 4–6 weeks</span>
        <h4>Making it stick</h4>
        <ul>
          <li>Structured accountability check-ins</li>
          <li>Practice, troubleshooting, and reinforcement</li>
          <li>Peer learning and shared problem solving</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="model-grid fade-in">
      <div class="model-card">
        <div class="model-card-head">CBO Selection</div>
        <div class="model-card-body">
          <ul>
            <li>Referrals from trusted ally organisations connected with CBOs</li>
            <li>4–5 CBOs per cohort</li>
            <li>3–5 hours time commitment per week from CBOs</li>
            <li>Minimal participation fee (₹500–₹2,000) to ensure commitment</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="model-card">
        <div class="model-card-head">Human Resource</div>
        <div class="model-card-body">
          <p>One youth intern (GenZ team member) assigned per cohort cycle, providing implementation support to 2 organisations to expand CBO capacity bandwidth.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
