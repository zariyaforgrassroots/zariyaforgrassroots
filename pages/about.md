      <h2>How we got here</h2>
      <p>
        ZariyaForGrassroots is a journey we started in May 2024, after collectively spending 15 years in the development sector and 23 years in professional work. The question at the heart of our work: <strong>How do we enable community-led organisations to thrive? How do we enable equity for CBOs in the nonprofit ecosystem?</strong>
      </p>
      <p>
        In 2024, we talked to multiple small-scale organisations across India working in agriculture, palliative care, education, media, and livelihoods. We listened to these founders at length and looked closely at their work. To support them, we designed workshop-style capacity building modules and one-on-one coaching calls.
      </p>
      <p>
        Through this, we realised the need for longer, sustained support to community-led organisations. This led to the design of the <strong>Saksham Finance Program</strong> in May 2025 — our first structured cohort, working with Van Gujjar Yuva Sansthan (Uttarakhand), Zep Foundation (Akola), and Pahal Shiksha Samiti (Bhopal).
      </p>
      <p>
        This program wrapped up in February 2026, leaving us with rich lessons and insights to design further. So far, we have self-funded our work. Through our experience over the last 1.5 years, our conviction in the urgency and need of this work has taken strong roots.
      </p>

      <blockquote>
        "You are able to sense where we are in our org journey. You speak in a way that our team from the community can understand."

      <h2 style="margin-top:3rem;">What we believe</h2>
      <p>
        Institutional fragility in CBOs is not a failure of commitment. It is the result of systemic design — of ecosystems built for organisations that already have English as an operating language, multi-year histories, and budgets above ₹20 lakh.
      </p>
      <p>
        Community institutions carry distinct strengths: deep contextual intelligence, intergenerational trust, participatory approaches, and lived experience of injustice. They design from within their communities.
      </p>
      <p>
        Yet proximity, passion, and legitimacy alone cannot sustain an institution. Without financial systems, communication infrastructure, and fundraising readiness, even the most committed organisations remain vulnerable. We exist to close that gap — through sustained, practice-based support built around each organisation's real needs.
      </p>
    </div>
